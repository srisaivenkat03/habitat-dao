Dual-Token System

HAT (Habitat Asset Token)

Fractional ownership in property and infrastructure. HAT tokens represent your stake in the community's real estate assets, appreciating in value as the community grows and property values increase.

HUSD (Habitat Stablecoin)

Used for daily transactions like rent, groceries, services. HUSD provides price stability for everyday transactions while keeping value within the community ecosystem.

Legal & Compliance

  • Wyoming DAO LLC structure providing legal recognition and liability protection
  • SEC, KYC/AML, and real estate regulation compliance ensuring investor protection
  • Token classification and liability protections under current regulatory framework
  • Partnership agreements with local municipalities and housing authorities

Implementation Roadmap

  1. Form DAO and launch tokens with initial community of 100 founding members
  2. Acquire land and build pilot community with 50 units in partnership with local government
  3. Deploy governance tools and establish community voting protocols
  4. Scale to multiple locations and integrate additional services like healthcare and education
  5. Measure and optimize impact through continuous data collection and community feedback
